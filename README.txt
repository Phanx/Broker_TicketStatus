Broker_TicketStatus
======================

Shows the status of your GM Help Tickets on your Broker display.

* Left-click to edit your ticket or submit a new one.
* Middle-click to abandon your ticket.
* Right-click for options.


Localization
---------------

Compatible with all game locales.

Translated into English, Deutsch, Español, Português, Русский,
简体中文 and 繁體中文.

Add or update translations on the [CurseForge project page] [1]:

	[1]: http://wow.curseforge.com/addons/broker-ticketstatus/localization/
